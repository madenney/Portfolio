About Matt

Back-end Web Developer with a repertoire of front-end skills.

Hello! Thanks for taking the time to see my site.

My name is Matt and I'm a Full Stack Web Developer. Here's my quick story:

My journey into web development started during my time at UC Davis, where I graduated with a degree in Bioinformatics.

While taking classes and working as a lab technician, I discovered that I preferred the computer science side over the biology side of my field.

So I decided to take some time to travel while I was still young and so I could figure out what I really wanted to do.

While abroad, I continued to work on several side projects and that inevitably led me into the world of web development.

When I returned to California, I saved up some money and I signed up for an accelerated learning web development program.

Fortunately, I found that I had a real passion for this field and I thrived in the web development environment.

I've spent hundreds of hours learning new skills and now I look forward to turning this passion into a fulfilling career.
